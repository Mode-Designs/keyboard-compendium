For the Sonnet's second generation, we refined the board without redrawing it. We kept the sculpted profile and accent bar people knew, and put our work into the internals, the acoustics, and new color material and finish options. We also collaborated with our new Chief Design Officer, Matthew Encina, on a white oak accent. That collaboration went so well it led to the development of Encore.
