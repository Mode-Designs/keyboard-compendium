![2026 Encore](../../assets/encore-2026.webp)

The Encore moved from a limited collaboration into our permanent lineup. It kept its mixed-material language: a split hardwood top plate in walnut, maple, or white oak over anodized aluminum, across five colorways. It was also the first of our boards to use the Crown Mounting System. Hi-fi-inspired spike feet and suede force-break pads kept unwanted sound characteristics out. It was the clearest expression yet of why we do this: the things you touch every day should be a joy to see, hear, and touch.
