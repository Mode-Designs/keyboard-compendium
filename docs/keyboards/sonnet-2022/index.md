`Status: Legacy` · `Production Years: 2022-2024` · `Layout: 75%`

![2022 Sonnet](../../assets/sonnet-2022.webp)

The Sonnet was our first 75%, and the shape most people picture when they think of Mode. We moved away from the simple lines of the Eighty and SixtyFive and gave it a sculpted side profile and a contrasting accent bar, this time screwed to the case instead of held by magnets. The body came in three parts you mixed and matched in our configurator, and a brass or copper weight could take a build past 7 pounds. The Sonnet did not have the same momentum as the SixtyFive at launch but over the years it became our most iconic design.
